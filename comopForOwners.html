Who is Comop?

Mauro and Jarne are two fitness enthusiasts who saw a problem in the fitness. Many people including friends of the two guys, stop working out because they have lack of motivation. In addition, there is a very cold atmosphere and there is a community feeling missing. This is what the boys wanted to change.

They proudly present comop, an initiative by fitness enthusiasts for fitness enthusiasts.
